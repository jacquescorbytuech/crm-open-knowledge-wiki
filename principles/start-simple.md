# Stance

Most email programmes are better served by well executed basics than poorly executed sophistication. Crawl, then walk, then run.

# In practice

The same holds for AI personalisation. For most brands the limiting factor is not that the bought machine learning lags the platforms; it is that the basics, clean data, a defined goal, and disciplined frequency, are not yet in place, and no vendor AI compensates for that. See [decisioning and personalisation](/foundations/decisioning-and-personalisation.md) and [customer data and identity](/foundations/customer-data-and-identity.md).
